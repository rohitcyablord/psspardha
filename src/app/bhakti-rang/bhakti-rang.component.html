Competition Rules

  1. Lenght of the song should be between 3 minute to 6 minute.
  2. Song can be in Marathi or Hindi language.
  3. Allowed Song types are:
    Bhakti geet, Abhanga, Gaulan, Bhajan
  4. Use of Kareoke track or recorded tracks is not allowed.
  5. Chrous singing is not allowed.
  6. Contestant can take help of accompanist for background music. Example of allowed musical instruments are :
    Harmonium, Tabla, Mrudanga, Pakhwaj, Khanjiri, Manjiri, Flute, Violin etc.
  7. Maximum allowed instruments are 5.
  8. Electronic musical instruments are not allowed. Except from Electric Tanpura.
  9. Video must be in landscape (horizantal) orientation. Videos shot in portrait (vertical) oriantation will be disqualified.
  10. View and Likes of first 15 days will be counted from the date of publication on our (Harmonium Prafulla) YouTube channel. Eg. If your video is published on 1st August then we will take Views and Like reading of your video on 15th August (9:00 PM).
